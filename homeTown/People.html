In Cardston, Alberta the people are diverse and willing to share their culture. Cardston is right beside one of the biggest reserves in Canada, Blood 148. Here in Cardston you will learn a lot about the First Nations culture like learning their dances and watching their princess pageants! There are also a lot of immigrants that come here for the nice peaceful town. Cardston is known to be a religious town. The population is a total of 3,348 so it is a nice sized town. The people are nice as well. Ask for directions are you will receive your answer.
