Cardston Is a home for many young families and tons of senior citizens where people walk the creeks path with their dog on a nice fall day. There are many tourist attractions and lots of events that come up during all seasons. We would encourage you to come to our small town full of friendly smiles and warm welcomes. Everyone enjoys the town as there are mountains and hills and beautiful sunsets and sunrises.
